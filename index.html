Control anything from your smartphone!

Tah is a BLE enabled, arduino compatible protyping board.
It is now easier than ever to connect your smart devices with everything around with Tah!

play

Emulate video game controls on your PC or PlayStation using the Tah Play App.

rgb

Make a mood lamp and change the lighting on a bunch of LEDs with the Tah RGB app.

motion

Use swipe gestures to control your mac's desktop and audio

sensor

The Tah Sensor app makes it easy to view the data relayed from various sensors directly on your phone.

Arduino

Use your existing Arduino shields with the help of the Breakout Shield

Gameduino Sheild
Neo Pixel Shield
